Reliability-Based Design By Dr. Pandey

Professor Pandey is a leading researcher and educator in the field of engineering risk and reliability analysis. He is internationally recognized for developing stochastic process models for time-variant reliability analysis. Prof. Pandey has been closely working with the nuclear power industry and leading a Research Chair Program on the risk assessment and life cycle management of nuclear facilities. His current research also includes stochastic modeling of reliability and resilience of infrastructure systems under the changing climate.

ACE Lecture Series

ACE, IIT Guwahati brings to you the lecture series based on novel 'Multi-Fidelity physics-informed deep neural network(MF-PIDNN)'. This framework proposed is particularly suitable when the physics of the problem is known in an approximate sense (low-fidelity physics), and only a little high-fidelity data is available.
This session was curated in association with Prof. Souvik Chakraborty is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Applied Mechanics at IIT Delhi He also holds a Joint Faculty position at the School of Artificial Intelligence at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.
