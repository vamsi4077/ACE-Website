Overview

The Association of Civil Engineering (popularly known as ACE) at IIT Guwahati, was established with a prime objective to proliferate knowledge & address industrial issues by bringing corporates, professors and students on common platform. ACE, having students as well as faculties as its members, is one of the most active organizations of civil engineers in the country.
We aim to promote Civil Engineering by providing the much-needed practical exposure to the community members through its regular activities like technical seminars, research symposium, talks on ongoing research practices throughout the globe and many other related topics from distinguished practitioners of the trade.
Collaboration between the school and industry is important for the advancement of engineering teaching and research. With this aim, to give our students some practical insight into Civil Engineering, we organize several visits throughout the year to ongoing construction sites thus giving them a chance to interact with key personnel of the industry.
